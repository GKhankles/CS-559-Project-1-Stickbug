Author: George Khankeldian
Project: CS 559 Programming Assignment 1

Description: The following project contains a circle of different color squares surrounding a stickbug.

Instructions:
-The first slider controls the amount of red in the squares.
-The second slider controls the amount of green in the squares.
-The third slider controls the amount of blue in the squares.
-The fourth slider controls the movement of the squares and the stickbug.

Note: The color of the squares is partially determined by the position of each respective square as well.
As a result, it may be difficult to obtain certain colors.
